The Challenge

Your society is losing thousands of litres — and lakhs of rupees

Conventional domestic RO purifiers create massive hidden costs for residents and RWAs alike.

01
Massive Water Wastage
A typical 92-flat society wastes 2,760 litres every single day from domestic ROs — that's 10 lakh litres per year worth ₹2.5 Lakhs.
02
Billing Nightmares
RWAs have no way to bill residents based on actual water consumption. A flat monthly levy creates friction and disputes.
03
STP Overload
RO reject water overburdens the Sewage Treatment Plant, causing frequent breakdowns and expensive emergency maintenance.
04
High Resident Spend
Each resident spends ₹7,000+ per year juggling between water cans (microplastics risk) and RO purifiers (high maintenance, disturbance).

The Process

From rooftop to your kitchen tap — in 7 steps

Our end-to-end installation is minimally invasive — think gas pipeline, not plumbing overhaul.

1
Water Quality Test
We analyse your inlet water source to design a custom filtration system specific to your area's water profile.
2
Custom System Design
Our engineers design a centralized RO plant sized perfectly for your apartment community's daily needs.
3
Rooftop Installation
Two Central RO plants + 500L anti-microbial storage tanks installed on the terrace. Zero disruption to residents.
4
Waste Water Recovery
Plumbing lines redirect softened RO reject water back to the water sump — saving up to 10,800 L/year per household.
5
Flexi Pipe Routing
A slim 16mm pipe runs through existing ducts to every home — like your gas line, no complex plumbing changes needed.
6
In-Home UV Dispenser
A Wi-Fi enabled UV point-of-use dispenser is installed in your kitchen. Choose normal or hot water at a tap.
7
Subscribe & Enjoy
Recharge monthly or annually via the DrinkPrime app. Live TDS tracking. Unlimited pure water. Done.
IoT Dashboard
RWA and residents get real-time water quality + consumption analytics via web dashboard and mobile app.
